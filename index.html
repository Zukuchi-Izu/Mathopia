REAL-TIME COMBAT OVERVIEW

Fight monsters in real-time by solving math challenges under pressure. Correct answers allow you to strike enemies down. Mistakes mean taking damage as monsters hit you back instantly. Every turn gives you exactly 22 seconds to choose your answer and keep your hero alive.

CHOOSE YOUR HERO

Knight

Features 30 total HP. Shields you from penalties by completely blocking the damage of your first wrong answer once during a fight.

Wizard

Reduces choice anxiety by limiting multiple choice prompts to just 2 answers instead of 3. Deals double damage with every single correct reply.

Rogue

Relies on chance when math skills fall short. Flips a coin on wrong selections to give you a solid 50% probability to dodge damage entirely.

Noble

Provides a buffer during tight encounters. Gives you one free retry per battle where an incorrect answer simply generates a brand new equation instead of hurting you.

Demon

Functions as a glass cannon with only 15 HP. Deals rapid damage by striking the current enemy twice on every correct answer.
